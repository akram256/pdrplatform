The PDR Platform

This is an interaction platform for product and process development professionals and engineers who want to know where to find facilities, processes, equipment and tools, and the technical personnel to support them, to create and manufacture high quality competitive goods and services for the large local market.

Many professionals and companies have access to some manufacturing processes and equipment, however, advances in technology mean that companies have to specialise and limit the scope of tools and processes they can economically utilise. A lot of manufacturing capacity is therefore available to be explored. This platform brings together these professionals and provides a forum to know what they can do for each other in line with new product and process development goals.

Processes & Professionals

Professionals, industrial process experts, industrial technicians, independent product developers and research students meet here to market their products, skills and services, creating an affordable mass technical market on this PDR platform. Join the PDR platform in one of four categories: Professionals with advanced engineering and analytical tools to mentor and guide the engineering teams. Industrial experts well versed with select manufacturing processes to guide the teams of professionals on the tools, equipment, processes and standards used in their industry. Product developers and students who need gainful career development, practical expertise and training while providing affordable and rewarding career work to the industrial and professional platform members. Manufacturing services providers the industries to provide manufacturing outsourcing services to the product developers.

Membership Benefits
  • New markets for industries with extra capacity as manufacturing services providers
  • Consultancy opportunities for professional and industrial experts on product development
  • Peer review on technical services quality assurance to improve product market survival
  • Growth of independent product developers with access to manufacturers and technical experts
  • Increased competitiveness with cost effective R&D technical services for low capital firms
  • Career and technical capacity advancement through product development and diversification
  • Jobs and self employment opportunities for young professionals with practical and soft skills
  • Interaction and expertise exchange to improve the capacity for complex engineering work
  • Links to manufacturing service providers to create vertical integrated value addition chains
